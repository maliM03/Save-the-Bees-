What's the problem?

Bees are a vital part of our ecosystem. They perform 80% of pollination worldwide, providing us with the food we eat and the flowers we love.

Sadly, bees are in need of our help. They have been declining in population since as early as 1962 from largely human causes.

These causes include: pesticides, drought, habitat destruction, nutrition deficit, air pollution, and global warming.

Luckily, we still have time to save our bees, so scroll down and find out how you can help!
